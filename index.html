Privacy Policy

Find The Number · What's The Number · Kids Learning Games
Last updated: April 2026

Short version: Our games do not collect, store, or share any personal information from children or anyone else. We have no accounts, no logins, and no data collection of any kind.

Who We Are

These games are developed independently and designed for children aged 2 to 8 years old. The games are educational number-learning games available on the Google Play Store.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs from children
  • Photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Any information from children under 13

Advertising

Our games display advertisements provided by Unity Ads. We use Unity Ads in compliance with Google Play's Families Policy. Unity Ads in our games:

  • Does not serve interest-based or personalised ads to children
  • Only shows age-appropriate content-based ads
  • Complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • Complies with GDPR requirements for children's apps

For Unity Ads privacy information please visit: unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy

Data Storage

All game progress (score, sound settings) is stored locally on your device only using Unity's PlayerPrefs system. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our games are designed for children and we take their privacy very seriously. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not require account creation or registration
  • No in-app purchases are available within the children's game sections
  • Parents do not need to provide any information to use our games

Third Party Services

Our games use the following third party service:

  • Unity Ads — for displaying advertisements. Unity's privacy policy applies to their ad serving. Unity Ads is certified for use in apps directed at children.

Permissions

Our games do not request any sensitive device permissions. No access to camera, microphone, contacts, location, or external storage is required.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
